Welcome to Our School Website

My School Academy is a prime educational facility that caters to lower primary school students. We provide the national curriculum that leads to Competency Based Curriculum (CBC), and enrich them with additional academic and extra-curricular subjects such as French, Information Technology, Environmental Science, Visual Arts, Music, Drama, Sports.

OUR VISION

To be a leadership resource center for Africa and beyond for generations to come.

OUR MISSION

To provide the finest education possible for future leaders by informing the mind, developing the body, and enlightening the spirit. We graduate scholars, artists, athletes, and community leaders.

PUPIL CARE

My School Academy prides itself on providing students with a strong foundation for a fulfilling and successful life. Our desire is to educate, nurture, and develop future leaders. Our education philosophy is anchored on the premise that every child is unique. As such, our programmes and practices are aligned towards enabling every learner attain excellence in what they are gifted in. The learners are therefore mentored and supported by an excellent team of staff members who are dedicated to their success, wellness, and well-being. At My School Academy you encounter a community of friendly, hardworking people who care for one another and whose sole purpose is to mould confident future leaders.
